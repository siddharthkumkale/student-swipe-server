Delete your account — Student Swipe

Last updated: 5 April 2026

You can request deletion of your Student Swipe account and associated personal data. This page explains how, in line with Google Play requirements.

Option 1 — Email (recommended)

  1. Open the email app on your phone or computer.
  2. Send an email from the same address you use to sign in to Student Swipe (for example your Google account email).
  3. Send it to: siddharthkumkale2@gmail.com
  4. Use a subject line such as: Student Swipe — account deletion request
  5. In the message, ask us to delete your account and data, and confirm the email address on the account.

Option 2 — From the app

In the Student Swipe app, open Settings and tap Delete account. Follow the prompts; this opens an email with a template you can send to finish the request.

What we delete

When we process a verified deletion request, we aim to remove or anonymize data associated with your account, including your profile, photos you uploaded, swipes and matches, chat messages tied to your account, and similar personal data held in our systems, subject to reasonable backup cycles and legal obligations that require retention.

How long it takes

We aim to complete requests within a reasonable time (often within a few business days after we can verify your identity via your sign-in email). Complex cases may take longer; we will confirm by email when processing is done where practicable.

After deletion

You may need to reinstall or sign out on any device. You can create a new account later using the same email only if our systems allow a fresh registration after deletion.

Questions

If you have trouble deleting your account or did not get a response:

Email: siddharthkumkale2@gmail.com
