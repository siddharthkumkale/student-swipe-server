Common topics

Sign-in problems

Student Swipe may use Google sign-in. Make sure Google Play Services is up to date, your connection is stable, and you’re selecting the correct Google account.

Profile or photos

Edit your profile under Settings → Edit profile. If a photo fails to save, try a smaller image or check your connection.

Notifications

Enable notifications in Settings → Notifications in the app and in your device settings for Student Swipe.

Location and map

Map features may ask for location permission. You can allow or deny in system settings; denying location may limit map behaviour.

Delete my account

Use Settings → Delete account in the app or follow the steps on our Delete account page.
